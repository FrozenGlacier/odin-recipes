Baby Spinach Omelette

Description

Eggs, baby spinach, Parmesan cheese and a bit of nutmeg are cooked to perfection. A great way to start the day!

Ingredients

Serves 1

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup baby spinach leaves, torn
  • 1 1/2 tsp Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 1/4 tsp onion powder
  • 1/8 tsp ground nutmeg
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a bowl, beat the eggs, then stir in the baby spinach and Parmesan cheese.
  2. Season with onion powder, nutmeg, salt and pepper.
  3. In a small pan coated with over medium heat, cook the egg mixture for about 3 minutes, until partially set.
  4. Flip with a spatula, then cook for a further 2 to 3 minutes.
  5. Reduce the heat to low, and continue cooking for 2 to 3 minutes or until cooked to your liking.
  6. Serve with toast and sliced avocado. Optionally, serve with rashers of bacon.
